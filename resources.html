Welcome to the University of Ghana Computer Science Department! We are thrilled to have you explore the exciting world of computer science with us. Our department is committed to fostering excellence in education and cutting-edge research.

Overview of the Computer Science Department:



Founded in 1947, the Computer Science Department at the University of Ghana stands as a pioneering institution at the intersection of education and innovation in the dynamic field of computer science. Over its extensive history, the department has consistently demonstrated a steadfast commitment to excellence, shaping the technological landscape through its educational initiatives and innovative contributions. With an illustrious legacy, the department takes pride in its pivotal role in the rapid evolution of technology. This commitment to staying at the forefront of advancements reflects in the department's ongoing contributions to the ever-changing realm of technology. As a hub of knowledge and expertise, the Computer Science Department remains dedicated to shaping the future of technology through cutting-edge research, quality education, and a forward-thinking approach.




Student Resources:



Your success is at the forefront of our priorities. We are dedicated to providing you with a wealth of resources and support to enhance your academic journey. Our academic advising services are tailored to guide you through your educational path, ensuring that you make informed decisions about your studies. For additional assistance, our tutoring programs are readily available, offering a supportive environment to deepen your understanding of the coursework. We believe in equipping you with the tools you need for success, and our comprehensive range of resources is designed to complement your learning experience. As you progress in your studies, we encourage you to explore internship opportunities that bridge the gap between theory and practice, providing invaluable real-world insights into the field of computer science. To further empower you in your career aspirations, our career services are here to offer guidance and support as you navigate the exciting possibilities in the world of computer science. Your journey is unique, and we are here to ensure that you have the resources and guidance to make the most of your academic and professional endeavors.
