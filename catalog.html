Programs Offered:


1. Bachelor of Science in Computer Science:

Dive into the foundations and advanced concepts of computer science, preparing yourself for a dynamic career in technology.


2. Master of Science in Computer Science:

Elevate your expertise with our comprehensive master's program, offering specialized tracks in areas such as artificial intelligence, cybersecurity, and data science.



3. Ph.D. in Computer Science:

Join our esteemed faculty in groundbreaking research and contribute to the advancement of knowledge in computer science.
