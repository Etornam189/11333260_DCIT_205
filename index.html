WELCOME TO THE UNIVERSITY OF GHANA, COMPUTER SCIENCE DEPARTMENT


we are committed to providing high-quality education and research in computer science


The department offers two undergraduate degree programs: Bachelor of Science in Computer Science Single-Major and Major-Minor as well as Bachelor of Science in Information Technology

The department also offers MPhil and Msc programs in Computer Science . The department provides short courses in various areas of computer science such as web programming, information security and ethical hacking, computer network fundamentals, and mobile application development .

The department’s website also has a section for upcoming and ongoing events organized by the department 1.

The Department of Computer Science at University of Ghana is a leading institution in the field of computer science education in Ghana. The department offers a range of programs, including undergraduate degrees, MPhil and Msc programs, and short courses. The undergraduate degree programs include Bachelor of Science in Computer Science Single-Major, Major-Minor, and Bachelor of Science in Information Technology. The department also offers MPhil and Msc programs in Computer Science. In addition, the department provides short courses in various areas of computer science such as web programming, information security and ethical hacking, computer network fundamentals, and mobile application development. The department’s website also has a section for upcoming and ongoing events organized by the department.
